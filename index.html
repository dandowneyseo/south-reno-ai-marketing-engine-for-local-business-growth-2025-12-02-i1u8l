Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI marketing engine

How quickly can we get started?

We can have your AI chatbot up and running in as little as 24-48 hours. After your initial consultation, our team will customize the chatbot to your specific business needs, integrate it with your website and communication channels, and train it on your products/services. Most clients see their first qualified leads within the first week.

Will the AI chatbot replace my customer service team?

No, the AI is designed to work alongside your team, not replace them. It handles initial inquiries, answers frequently asked questions, and qualifies leads—freeing your team to focus on high-value activities like closing sales and providing exceptional service. Most businesses use the time savings to expand their service offerings or focus on client retention rather than reducing staff.

What happens if the AI doesn't know how to answer a question?

Our AI is trained to recognize when a question is outside its knowledge base and will politely escalate to a human team member. The conversation is seamlessly transferred with full context, so your team can pick up exactly where the AI left off. This ensures customers always get the help they need while maintaining a professional experience.

How do you keep customer data secure?

Security is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL) for all data in transit and at rest. Our infrastructure is GDPR and CCPA compliant, and we conduct regular security audits. All customer data is stored on secure, redundant servers with automatic backups. We never sell or share customer data, and you maintain complete control over your information.

What's the cost and how is it calculated?

Pricing is based on your business size and the number of conversations your AI handles monthly. We offer flexible plans starting at an affordable rate for small businesses, with volume discounts for larger operations. Most clients see ROI within 30-60 days through increased lead volume and conversion rates. We're happy to discuss pricing options during your free consultation—there are no hidden fees or long-term contracts.
